Top Attractions

San Luis Potosí Cathedral

Located in the heart of the Historic Center, this cathedral is one of the city's most iconic landmarks. Built in the 17th century, it features impressive Baroque architecture and overlooks Plaza de Armas, making it a must-see destination for visitors.

Parque Tangamanga I

One of the largest urban parks in Mexico, Parque Tangamanga offers walking trails, lakes, museums, sports facilities, and green spaces. It's a favorite spot for families, runners, and anyone looking to relax outdoors.

Plaza del Carmen & Teatro de la Paz

Plaza del Carmen is one of the most beautiful plazas in the city. It is home to the historic Teatro de la Paz, the National Mask Museum, and the Templo del Carmen. The plaza is a major cultural gathering place and hosts important events throughout the year.
